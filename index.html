Hi everybody!


My name is Abby Scharf and I live in Boston, Massachusents! I'm a true Bostonian and have been living in Boston my whole life. Boston is such an amazing city, and I love it so much. Some of my favorite things about Boston are the trains, the duck boats and sail boats on the Charles River, the amazing skyline, the sports teams and so much more.





Boston will always be where my heart is no matter what. Boston is such a special place to me and I know that where ever I go it will always be my home. No matter where I live, I will always be rooting for Boston. Once a Red Socks fan, always a Red Socks.

Besides for walking around Boston, one of my favorite things to do are jigsaw puzzles!

Ever since I was little, I have always loved math, puzzles and everything related. One of my favorite things to do since I was a little kids is building jigsaw puzzles! I started out by building small 1,000 piece puzzles, and I worked my way up from there.






Things really stared to get intresting when my cousins bought me an 18,000 piece puzzle for my 12th birthday! I was so excited and imediatly got to work. My parents thought that this puzzle would last me years; however, little did they know that this huge puzzle would only take me 3 years to finish.






After complteing an 18,000 piece puzzle, my parents then bought me a 32,000 piece puzzle for my 15th birthday. To this day I am still working on this puzzle, but I hope to finish it soon and go on to complete even bigger puzzles!
